<p>
<label for="T48_5">Adenosine stress protocol:</label>
<textarea id="T48_5" name="Adenosine stress protocol">0.14 mg/kg/min adenosine infused over 4 minutes with stress perfusion imaging performed during the last minute of the adenosine infusion using an FGRE-ET sequence and a gadolinium dose of 0.1 mmol/kg. Rest perfusion imaging was performed was then performed after appropriate delay using additional gadolinium dose of 0.1 mmol/kg. Cine white blood imaging including short axis and two, three and four chamber long axis views was performed using a FIESTA sequence to evaluate cardiac morphology and function. Delayed enhanced imaging was performed using an Inversion recovery prepared gradient echo sequence. Images were postprocessed on a computer workstation to assess cardiac anatomy and ventricular function. </textarea>
</p>
