The area was prepped with sterile cleansing solution. The retention suture was removed. While pinching the chest tube, the catheter was cut to disengage the pigtail. While the patient was holding their breath on inhalation and bearing down, the chest tube was removed. A vasoline occlusive dressing was applied over the insertion site.  The patient's oxygen saturation, as measured by pulse oximetry, remained stable throughout the procedure.</textarea>
